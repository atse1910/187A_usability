09. Error Recovery

Error Recognition and Recovery

Error messages should be expressed in plain language (no codes), precisely indicate the problem, and constructively suggest a solution.
— Jacob Nielsen

When users do come across errors, users are informed of this error through a personalized 404 web page. However, University of Louisiana at Lafayette's website is not specific as to what kind of error caused the problem. This kind have some impact on a user's ability to be efficent when knowing what problems is present.
