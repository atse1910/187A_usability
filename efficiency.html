07. Efficiency

Flexibility and Efficiency of Use

Accelerators -- unseen by the novice user -- may often speed up the interaction for the expert user such that the system can cater to both inexperienced and experienced users. Allow users to tailor frequent actions.
— Jacob Nielsen

This website shows all the sub-categories under the higher-level menu. It seems a little messy, yet it is efficient. For example, when we tried to go to page that contains “Study Abroad”, we only needed one single click to “Current Student” from “Home”. Since the site-map like “Current Student” shows that “Study Abroad” is located under “Academic Programs”. It saves users a lot of time, and reduces the unnecessary rummaging of the menu and unhelpful pages just to see if it contains the material they are looking for. We found that in order to go to the page called “Study Abroad” on the UCSD website, we needed to follow four pathways: “Current”, “Student Life”, “International”, and “Program Abroad”. To add on, we experienced that we were clicking other menus such as “Academics” and “Department & Programs” to check it they contained the contents.
Side bars are one of the most powerful options that make using websites more efficiently. However, we found that the side menu of this website has a big problem. Firstly, we need to think about the main function of a side bar in websites. When users surf websites, the side bar is the first thing they check because it is always present, no matter what page they are viewing.
In this website they put the very basic menus such as “Prospective Students”, “Current Students”, “Alumni”, “Visitors”, etc on the main menu. From the user’s point of view, only one of the menus fits their profile. Once they have clicked on the menu that is relevant to them, it is unnecessary for the contents of the main menu to be on the side bar.
In conclusion, having the main menu options on the side bar is actually a waste of a space that could be used more effectively. We recommend editing this system to a drop down menu for easier navigation of the website.
