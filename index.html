Home Page

Welcome to our Usability Evaluation of University of Louisiana at Lafayette. On this site, our goal is to present you the applicability of Jacob Nielsen's 10 Heuristics of Usability for the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. We will approach this website using holistic methods evaluating this website from first impressions to overall user-friendless. We tried to evaluate the University of Louisiana at Lafayette's website to the best of our ability using various attributes on the University of Louisiana at Lafayette website. Feel free to leave comments or feedback for each category of our analyses on the category's respective page.
