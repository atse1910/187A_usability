02. Match

         Language

         Acronyms



"Users often choose system functions by mistake and will need a clearly marked "emergency exit" to leave the unwanted state without having to go through an extended dialogue. Support undo and redo." --Nielsen
Language: It seems appropriate language. There is no reason to use 'Cajun Card' instead of '(ID/debit Card)'. It could be simply written by ID/debit card. Even the 'Cajun Card' is the name of student card such as triton card, they should have written the description of the name for people who are not studying at Univertsity of Louisiana.
Acronyms: This site provides lots of acronyms. Thankfully, it also provides what acronyms stand for using parenthese, for example, Student Support Services (SSS), Upward Bound (UB), and Veterans Upward Bound (VUB).
